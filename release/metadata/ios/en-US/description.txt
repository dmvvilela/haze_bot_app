A living robot face that teaches kids emotions — and the cutest thing on your desk.

Prop up your phone, plug it in, and Haze comes alive: two big glowing eyes that blink, breathe, follow your finger, and act out real feelings. Poke it and it chirps. Long-press it and it sings.

THE FEELINGS GAME
Haze makes a face — happy, sad, scared, surprised... — and your kid picks the matching word. Right answers earn chirps, streaks, and heart-eyed celebrations; wrong guesses get a gentle "look again", never a buzzer. Ten emotions, in English and Portuguese, made for early learners and kids practicing emotion recognition — including kids who find faces tricky to read.

TALK TO HAZE (OPTIONAL)
